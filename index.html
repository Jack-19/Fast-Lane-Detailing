Who Are We

Fast Lane Detailing is a world-class car detailing company with one goal – to ensure every car is cleaned ‘better than new’.

The team have a commitment to excellence, they use the finest available products and the service is as consummately professional as it is exclusive.

Fast Lane Detailing caters for everyday cars, sports and prestige models, garage kings and concourse queens as well as the new breed of hypercars.

Expert-level, bespoke car detailing requires a delicate combination of patience, passion and skill.

New technology, techniques and cutting-edge science are employed to make sure your car is perfect from every angle, inside and out, top to bottom.
